WHAT IS BOOK-ED?


This is an application to help students buy and sell books. It is a safe space that is specific for Anna University students. Our AuBookedBot will help you navigate throughout our application right from the comfort of your phones! With the click of a button, have the book you need Book-Ed! Get the details of the owner of the book for further communication and negotiation! Remember to be respectful to your fellow college mates!
